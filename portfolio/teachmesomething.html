Teach Me Something

Code: lksevans12/teachmesomething

Currently in progress

Teach Me Something is the first web app I built on my own after Dev Bootcamp. I describe it as 'wikipedia, but social'. The idea is that people can post informational articles in different categories -- similar to Wikipedia, however, readers will have the ability to interact with the authors, or as I call them 'teachers'. You need to specify on your profile page that you want to write articles, otherwise your profile will show you as a 'student'.

My next steps on this project are to make it responsive for desktop, tablet and mobile. I would also like to add the ability to ask a question to the author, and have the author answer. Also, I would like to implement the ability to add more than one picture to a post or profile. Building out an instant messaging system like I did for spcs, is also something that I thought about implementing.

The hardest part for me is implementing threads for the questions and responses. I have looked into best ways to build them, however, I'm having trouble storing all of the threads in the database. I plan to continue my research and to build a small sample app implementing threads for practice before building it on my app.

Current Technologies: Rails, PostGres, Active Record, CSS3, HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Paperclip, AWS
