Even Steven

During my time as the Interactive Intern for Resource Ammirati, An IBM Company the interns were asked to take lead of a full company rebranding. We had an Account Intern, an Art Director Intern, a Strategy Intern, a Project Management Intern and myself. We presented the client with 3 different personas for the company. The client picked the Even Steven persona -- bold, flamoyant and bright. We came up with a new business model, logo, packaging and website.

The original company, Levelclips, was selling primarily on an outdated website which didn't promote the unique technology of the the hanging hardware. The Interactive team chose to use Shopify to build the platform on. The Art Director designed a mock-up of the site. After client approval, I began building.

We used a pre-built theme as our platform, and I implemented custom CSS, HTML and JavsScript. The trickiest part of this project for me was implementing the Scroll Magic library. The 'Keep it Straight' section, we break up the parts of the hanging hardware to show consumers how the product works. I struggled to figure out how to get the level and top piece of the hardware to move together. Eventually, I got it to work, and was really able to caputre how the product works.
