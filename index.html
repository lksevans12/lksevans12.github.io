About me

Hi, I'm Laura!

My interest in programming began in 2014. I took a basic IT/Web class in college, and found it truly interesting. For my senior capstone project, I chose to build a website for a small head hunting firm. However, since I took a very basic class and my skills were extremely limited, I was unable to build a fully functioning website, and instead, built a basic mockup based on the client's needs.

In June 2014, I graduated from DePaul University with a BA in Communication and Media, with a minor in Journalism. Shortly after I graduated, my family had an emergency, and required me to stay home full-time. Boredom shortly struck, and I began taking beginner-programming classes to keep myself busy. After the first intro to HTML/CSS class I took with Women Who Code, I realized programming was something that I wanted to pursue. I began attending more classes with Girl Develop It (GDI), and was introduced to Dev Bootcamp (DBC).

As of Dec 2015, I am a graduate of DBC and a certified junior web developer.

Please make sure to check out my projects and my skillset.

Thanks for stopping by!
